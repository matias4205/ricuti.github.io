Passage of units

It´s a simple operation that you must have well oiled since you will use it very often.

There are at least three methods to do this operation.

1) Non-innocent multiplication by 1
2) Unit equivalents
3) Multiplicaton by conversion factors

You must learn all three of them and then use the one that suits you better.

Here you have all this conversions, done using the three methods. Click on each method..

  1) convert                20 m/s to km/h
  2) convert                3.500 km/h² to m/s²
  3) convert                0,7 N/m to kgf/cm
  4) convert                60.000  J/día to W
